## Number bases Summative quiz

1. `Convert the binary number (1111) into decimal`

- 15
- F
- 10001010111
- Not possible (in decimal, the numbers from 0-9 are used)

<details>

  <summary>Answer</summary>

  <br>

11000

</details>

  <br>

2. `Convert the the decimal number (222) into binary`

- 11011110
- 1111001
- 1010101
- Not possible

<details>

  <summary>Answer</summary>

  <br>

11011110

</details>

  <br>
