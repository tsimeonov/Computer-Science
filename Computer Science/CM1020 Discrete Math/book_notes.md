`Definition 2`

Let *p* and *q* be propositions. The *conjunction* of *p* and *q*, denoted by *p ∧ q*, is the proposition  
**"p and q."** The conjunction *p ∧ q* is true when both *p* and *q* are true and is false otherwise.
